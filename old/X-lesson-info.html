Lesson Information

If this is your first lesson, please print and complete a medical form (see: ‘Booking Forms’ LINK HERE TO ADD).

PRE-SCHOOL LESSONS (2.5 - 4 years)

A 20 minute lesson with a teacher in the water (2 children per teacher maximum; no parent in the water). Toys will be used for enjoyment and to encourage arm and leg movements, depending on age and ability.

Suitable for children aged 2.5 – 4 years.

LEARN TO SWIM (4+ years)

A 20 minute lesson with a teacher in the water (4 children per teacher maximum).

Suitable for children of school age and upwards.

Aims

  • Water Confidence
  • Ability to swim unaided
  • Introduce stroke work when ready

STROKE TECHNIQUE (4+ years)

A 30 minute lesson with a teacher out of the water (6 children per teacher maximum). Suitable for children with a basic knowledge of all four strokes.

MINI-SQUAD SESSIONS (8+ years)

A 1 hour lesson (10 swimmers per teacher maximum). Suitable for good swimmers aged 8 and above who:

  • Feel that they may like to join a swimming club.
  • Are in a swimming club but would like extra technique work.
  • Love to swim to keep fit.

ADULT LESSONS

Available on request.

INDIVIDUAL LESSONS (4+ years)

Suitable for children of school age and upwards. All abilities welcome.

BADGE SCHEME

Throughout the children’s swimming lessons they will work towards building stamina and confidence, and improving their stroke technique. Swimmers will work towards distance and technique badges. Waves operates a continual assessment programme, and awards badges at the end of every full term (3 times per year).
