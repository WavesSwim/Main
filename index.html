Message from Waves

Hello everyone, I hope that during these difficult times you are staying safe and well.

In light of the government and Swim England releasing guidelines on how swimming pools can start to reopen, we are working hard behind-the-scenes to ensure that we are well-prepared to meet the guidelines once a restart date has been agreed.

The health and safety of our swimmers, their families and our poolside teachers is our number-one priority. We are working alongside the school and using Swim England's advice to be in the position to continue to deliver high-quality lessons, whilst adhering to enhanced hygiene and cleaning routines. We will maintain strict social distancing throughout all areas of our operation.

Waves have been in close discussions with the school over the last few months regarding a start-back date, and have recently been advised that the earliest date for any third party hirer will be October of this year.

We will keep you fully informed throughout this process once everything is in place. In the meantime, stay safe and well, and enjoy the summer.

Caroline and the Waves team

Waves Swim School

Quality swimming lessons in Bristol

Waves Swim School (founded 1998) provides swimming lessons to hundreds of children across Bristol, and has developed an excellent reputation for its high standard of teaching.

Waves Swim School have an experienced team of teachers, all holding ASA and RLSS qualifications, with full child protection and DBS coverage. We have a range of specialisms, to be able to teach swimmers and non-swimmers of all ages and abilities.

Waves Swim School strongly believes that children benefit from learning to swim in a fun, safe and structured way. Our ‘Beginner’ and ‘Learn-To-Swim’ lessons are small (taking a maximum of 4 children), with each child receiving the attention they need to develop core skills at their own pace, assisted by teachers in the water. Following the ‘Beginner’ and ‘Learn-To-Swim’ classes, they will learn more technical stroke work in all four strokes, and develop stamina and good form.

Waves Swim School can offer one-to-one lessons for adults and children on request. Swimmers are continually assessed, and progress at a rate suited to their ability.

For full details of all the classes available, please see the Timetables page.
