Payments & Fees

PAYMENT DETAILS

Lessons run only in school term time. Fees will be determined by amount of weeks in the term. Families with 3 or more children attending swimming lessons will receive 50p per lesson per child reduction.

Renewals will be sent out via email at the end of each term. Waves accept cash or cheque, but our preferred method of payment is online. Details will be found on your booking form.

Note that late payments will incur a fee of £10.00

Please (Contact Waves) if you have any questions.
